Project 1

Dungeon Escape Playthrough

Project Overview

A group project, with 5 people involved, where we were tasked on creating a vertical slice of a game based on the theme of 'Escape'. Dungeon Escape is an isometric game that is based on death, once the player dies they will respawn with a power-up. The character had 5 different powers, allowing for gameplay to be expanded after every death.

I was the main programmer for this game and was tasked with creating a lot of the mechanics of the game. I coded the character movement, combat and animations, the enemy AI, dealing and taking damage, a point system that allows the character to pass through levels as they reach a certain threshold, the in-game UI and the power-ups. This was all done with Unreal Engine's blueprints system.

Throughout the video you can see the character has multiple movement and attack animations. When the player dies they will respawn with a new power-up and keep going until they clear the game, or die on the fourth attempt, resulting in a game over, making you restart from the beginning. The four power-ups the player gets are flashlight, increase movement speed, increase damage, and finally a pet that will heal the player when on low health. To get past the doors, you have to kill enough enemies to get a certain amount of points. Once you get enough, the points will reset back to zero and the doors will open.
